About Us

Location

We are conveniently located in central Kristianstad with great commuter options for visiting clients. Please consult the map in the “contact”-section for exact location.

History

The idea that became Incarnate Sound Studios, is a fairly long and winding story. It all began in the early nineties where some friends from school got together and made music on amiga-computers, nurturing the dream of that some day start an own recording studio. The early setup of the studio was pretty lo-fi and mostly “analog” in the sense that most recording was done with vintage equipment e. g. hardware synths, analog mixing desk. As digital tools became more accessible in the 2000’s the recording and mixing techniques became more computer-based and the studio nowadays has an “in the box”-outfit, while still implementing both the vintage setup as well as modern tools.

Vision

Our aim is to help aspiring artists, musicians and performers realize their sound and be able to deliver high quality recordings that meets the standard of modern music. We offer services from recording/ tracking of vocals and instruments to mixing and mastering of individual songs and albums.
